|----------|-------------|

| Build tooling → distributed binary | npm/Cargo dependencies become part of the shipped installer |

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-01-02 | Tampering | `package.json` npm supply chain | mitigate | Commit `package-lock.json`; use only official `@tauri-apps/*` and shadcn-canonical packages; no third-party shadcn registries (per 01-UI-SPEC §Registry Safety) |
| T-01-03 | Information Disclosure | Tauri capabilities over-grant | mitigate | This plan only grants `core:*` minimum permissions; Plan 02 adds `sql:*`, `dialog:allow-open`, `store:*` narrowly — no `fs:*` blanket permissions |
