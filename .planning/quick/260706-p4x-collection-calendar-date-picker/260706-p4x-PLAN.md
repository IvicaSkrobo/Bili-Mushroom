Replace the cumbersome dd/mm/yyyy (and mm/yyyy, dd/mm) digit-typing date filter inputs in the Collection tab's Filters popover with a real calendar picker — per user feedback: "date choosing for filtering is cumbersome, can it be a date picker like opens a calendar I can move months and days around and see days within month, month within year."
