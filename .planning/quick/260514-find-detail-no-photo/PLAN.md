# Plan

## Objective
Ensure clicking any find opens a detail panel, including finds without photos.

## Steps
1. Inspect collection/species find click handlers and PhotoLightbox usage.
2. Add or reuse a detail panel that accepts a Find without requiring photos.
3. Route no-photo clicks to the detail panel while preserving photo lightbox behavior where useful.
4. Show date, location, fruiting body count, notes, and species metadata.
5. Run focused build/tests.
