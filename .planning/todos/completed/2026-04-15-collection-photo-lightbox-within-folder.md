## Description

In the Collection tab, when browsing photos within a folder (species group), add a lightbox-style viewer to see photos larger — not full-screen, but an expanded in-app overlay showing one photo at a time with prev/next navigation.

## Context

Currently photos in collection folders are small thumbnails. User wants to review finds photo by photo within a folder without leaving the collection view.

## Acceptance

- Click photo → opens enlarged overlay (modal, not full-screen)
- Prev/next navigation through all photos in that folder
- Shows find notes / date alongside photo
- Dismissable (Esc or click outside)
